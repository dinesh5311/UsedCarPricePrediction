This Used Car Price Prediction model predict the approx price of an used car. The prediction is done on the basis of various criteria like manufacturing year of car ,engine type ,kilometer driven etc.The dataset of used car is taken from kaggle. By using Machine Learning, the dataset is trained and test in 8:2 ratio. We use Random Forest Regressor Model to predict the price of used car. This model gives about 84 % of accuracy ,which is good enough.
